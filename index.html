Class 12 Physics Formula Book

1. Electrostatics

Coulomb's Law: F = k \frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2}
Electric Field: E = F / q
Electric Potential: V = kQ / r

2. Current Electricity

Ohm's Law: V = IR
Resistance in Series: R = R₁ + R₂ + ...
Resistance in Parallel: 1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...

3. Magnetic Effects of Current

Biot-Savart Law: B = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \cdot \frac{Id\sin\theta}{r^2}
Ampere's Circuital Law: \oint B\cdot dl = \mu_0 I

4. Electromagnetic Induction

Faraday's Law: emf = - d\phi/dt
Lenz's Law: Direction of induced emf opposes the cause

5. Alternating Current

Peak Voltage: V₀ = I₀R
RMS Voltage: V = V₀ / \sqrt{2}

6. Optics

Lens Formula: 1/f = 1/v - 1/u
Magnification: m = h'/h = v/u

7. Modern Physics

Photoelectric Equation: E = hf - \phi
de Broglie Wavelength: λ = h/p
